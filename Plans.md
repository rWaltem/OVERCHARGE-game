# Gameplay Loop ideas

## Movement 

- Slight air control
- Charge boost at start
- Characters have a specialty track type and they have a speed boost when on their specialty track
- Drifting corners turns faster

## Race Types

- Touring
  - Race 4 tracks in a cup
- Battle
  - Same as touring but with items
- Time Trails
  - No items or other players, just get the best time
- Online
  - Online version of touring or battle

## Track Type

- Mobius
- Speed
- Inclosed
- End-to-end

## Ship Stats
- Speed
- Accel
- Weight
- Handling

### Hidden Stats
- Specialty speed boost
- Max Charge
- Recharge rate
- Recover speed

## Charge

- Ship has charge
- Ships have different max charges
- Charge Pads recharge ship
- If charge is full, charge pads give boost
- Charge can be used for temp boost

## 'Tricks'

- Tricks use charge for landing boost
- Roll left/right
  - Slightly rotates and translates ship in direction of roll
- Forward trick (no idea for animations rn)
  - Gives forward boost in air

## Items

- Bombs
- Missile
- Heat tracking missile
- Charge pack
- Discharge bomb
